As you may have heard in the news, Comcast is launching an XFINITY WiFi Home Hotspot network in several cities throughout the country. Comcast XFINITY® customers in these impacted areas, who lease wireless Internet routers in their homes, may be subjected to providing public Wi-Fi. In other words, Comcast XFINITY Internet customers will have their Wi-Fi signal split, with the second signal available for use of anyone within range. Comcast says users have the ability to opt out, though there is much discussion about this in community blogs.

Some Comcast customers are asking questions like:

  • “Why should a device in my home become a publicly available service?”
  • “Should I be worried about my privacy?”
  • “Is this secure?”
