3 Star Review for High-Speed Internet® 40Mbps

Albuquerque, NM

High-Speed Internet® 40Mbps

I installed this myself, there was a bit of confusion on my part, being new to the area, and never having a cable company not come out to install. I have issues with having to turn my modem off in order to get my signal back (computer will say my network is connected, but I am not able to access the internet). I have not had an issue in the past with such spotty reception. Otherwise, this seems to be a good company.
