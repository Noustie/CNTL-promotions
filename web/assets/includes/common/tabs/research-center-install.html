Can you install CenturyLink® High-Speed Internet service yourself?

The short answer is yes. But, you'd be better off letting a CenturyLink Tech handle the installation to make sure your Internet performs up to its full potential. The long answer is, even if you feel comfortable handling the installation, a CenturyLink Tech Install may still be necessary in certain circumstances:

  • If your previous provider was a cable company, as we'll need to reconnect the CenturyLink phone box to your inside wiring.
  • If your home was built before 1980, since an older home's wiring doesn't support maximum High-Speed Internet speeds.
  • If you live in an apartment building.

Let a CenturyLink Technician install your Internet service – it's important for maximum performance.

You know your way around modems and routers, and you've probably installed your own Internet service before. But it's still a good idea to let us handle the installation. That way you can be sure you're getting the highest performance possible without unforeseen factors – like the condition of your home's inside wiring and jacks – deteriorating your service.
