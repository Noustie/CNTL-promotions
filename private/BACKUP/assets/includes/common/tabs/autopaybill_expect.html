Enrolling in AutoPay is easy!

All you need is your account information and the payment source you wish to use.

Your AutoPay enrollment options are:
  • AutoPay with electronic funds transfer (EFT) from a checking or savings account.

    • For bank account enrollment, you'll need your routing and bank account number, which can be found on your check as shown below.

  • AutoPay with a credit or debit card.

    • For credit or debit card enrollment, you'll need your card information.

Once you register for AutoPay:
  • You'll receive an e-mail to confirm your enrollment.
  • Your first automatic recurring payment will occur 1-2 billing cycles after the confirmation e-mail is sent.
  • To check if your current bill will be paid through AutoPay, sign in to My Account or consult your bill.
