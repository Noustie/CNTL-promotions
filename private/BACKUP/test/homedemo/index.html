Security comes standard.

CenturyLink® Smart Home is more than a security system. It’s a state-of-the art home management system designed to monitor and protect the things that matter most, all from your smart phone, tablet and computer.

  • Arm and disarm at the touch of a button
  • REMOTE VIDEO MONITORING
  • ELECTRONIC DOOR LOCKS
  • Motion Detection

With CenturyLink® Smart Home, you can rest easy knowing that your home is connected to a CenturyLink monitoring center with the ability to dispatch essential services in the event of an emergency.

Use less. Save more.

Keep your energy bills under control by programing your system to heat and cool your home according to your family’s schedule. Or when your schedule changes, simply make an adjustment on the fly to ensure the whole family stays comfortable while still conserving energy. Text and email alerts mean you can monitor your family’s energy usage, even when your away, to guarantee there are no surprises on the electricity bill.

Manage your home on the road.

Life doesn’t always go according to plan. But CenturyLink® Smart Home gives you the power to manage your home the moment things change. Adjust thermostats, control door locks and lights from your smart phone, tablet or computer. All thanks to the fast, reliable CenturyLink High-Speed Internet service you already have.

Choose the package that's right for your family and add CenturyLink® Smart Home to your High-Speed Internet package.

With CenturyLink® Smart Home, installation is easy.

Once you have chosen a CenturyLink® Smart Home package, a representative will contact you to schedule a time for one of our qualified technicians to come  install your new system.

The technician will remove or integrate any existing alarm system components, mount your digital keypad and install protection points throughout your home. Be sure to talk to your installer about any special security or installation needs.

Once the hardware is in place, the installer will activate the alarm and give you a tutorial on getting started with your new system.

The CenturyLink Smart Home system requires a one-time installation fee of $299 and a one-time equipment fee that ranges from $199-$499.
