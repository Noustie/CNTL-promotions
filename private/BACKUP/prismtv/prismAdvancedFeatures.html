Find-It-Fast Navigation®

Every actor. Every title. Every director.

The toughest part about watching TV is finding something you actually want to watch, right? Well, not anymore. With Find-It-Fast Navigation®, you can find shows three different ways – by actor, title, and even director. So, if you love movies with a specific actor, all you have to do is type in their name, and every show that’s playing with them in it will pop up. Looking for a specific movie – same thing. Just type in the name of the movie, and bam, there it is.

Multi-View

4 shows. 1 screen.

Picture-in-picture navigation is great, but being able to watch four shows at the same time is even better. With CenturyLink® Prism™ TV, you get our newest feature Multi-View which lets you keep an eye on four different shows in a specific genre – all on one screen – at one time. So imagine watching four news shows at once. Or stopping your kids from fighting by letting them watch their four favorite kids’ shows at the same time. Multi-View makes all that possible with just the click of your remote.
