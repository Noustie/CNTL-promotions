  • Prism™ TV Overview

      |  TV on your terms.

    CenturyLink® Prism™ TV is more than interactive TV – it’s revolutionary. And it will change the way you watch TV forever. That’s why we’ve created the following videos to help give you a feel for everything Prism™ TV has to offer. From Prism’s numerous interactive features to Prism on the Go and our new Wireless Set-Top Box, you're finally free to watch what you want, where you want, and on any device you want, too.

  • Whole Home DVR

      |  1 DVR rules your whole home.

    It’s a single DVR for your entire home. Not four. One. That’s what you get when you sign up for CenturyLink® Prism™ TV – a Whole Home DVR that lets you watch and record up to four shows – even pause live TV – in any room. Which is great if you want to grab a mid-show snack. And you can also start a show in one room, pause it, and then pick it up in another room. So now you have freedom to take your recorded shows to any Prism-connected TV in your home.

  • Wireless Set-Top Box

      |  Put your TV anywhere - even in the backyard.

    If you thought Prism™ TV couldn’t get any better, it has. Now you can put your TV wherever you want – in the kitchen, living room, even the backyard – because our set-top boxes have gone wireless. So you’re free to design your rooms – even rooms that aren’t technically rooms – however you want.

  • Warp-Speed Channel Change

      |  Fly through channels one by one.

    Have you ever had to wait for your TV to catch up to your remote? Well, with Warp-Speed Channel Change that’s not an issue anymore. Why? Because when you get CenturyLink® Prism™ TV you’ll be able to fly from one channel to the next with virtually no lag time between channels.

  • Find-It-Fast Navigation®

      |  Actor. Title. Director. Pick one and it’ll find it.

    With Find-It-Fast Navigation®, you can find exactly what you want to watch three different ways – by actor, title, and even director. So if you love movies with a specific actor all you have to do is type in their name and every show that’s playing with them in it will pop up. Looking for a specific movie you heard is on – same thing. Just type in the name of the movie and bam, there it is.

  • Prism App Center

      |  Your smartphone now has competition.

    If you’re like most people, you rely on your smart phone almost as much as you rely on your computer – maybe even more. Which is why we created a new feature that takes the functionality of your smart phone and brings it to your TV. With the Prism App Center, you can access all your favorite social networking sites like Facebook, as well as Yahoo! Sports, Picasa, and more. You can get local news, sports, gas prices, recipes, your horoscope and play Sudoku, too.*
    * All marks are property of their respective owners.

  • Multi-View

      |  4 shows. 1 screen.

    Picture-in-picture navigation is great, but being able to watch four shows at the same time is even better. With CenturyLink® Prism™ TV, you get our newest feature, Multi-View, which lets you keep an eye on four different shows in a specific genre – all on one screen – at one time. So imagine watching four news shows at once. Or stopping your kids from fighting by letting them watch their four favorite kids’ shows at the same time. Multi-View makes all that possible with just the click of your remote.
