Not all DVRs are created equal.

1 DVR rules them all.

Record 4 shows at once with a single DVR.

With one DVR for your entire home, you can record up to four shows at once with a single DVR. Upstairs or downstairs, you can also access all your recorded shows and even manage live TV from any room in your home. And up to four people can watch the same recorded show, or different recorded shows, at the same time in different rooms. That means, you can start a show in one room, pause it, and finish it another room. There’s no more fighting over the remote, with Prism™ TV’s Whole Home DVR.






Manage Live TV!

Pause live TV from any room.

Live TV is now at your command. Now you can manage live TV from any room in your house. You can even pause a live show without extra equipment!





Manage your DVR remotely.

Even if you’re on the go, you won’t miss your show.

Working late? Out of town? With Prism’s Whole Home DVR, you’ll be able to manage you DVR library, set recordings and reminders, and customize your online guide from any Internet-enabled PC.



HD Ready.

Prepare yourself for reach-out-and-touch-it TV.

Prism’s Whole Home DVR is HD ready so you can see your shows the way they were meant to be seen. And because Prism uses fiber-optic† technology, the picture and sound will be unbelievably brilliant. Add HD service at any time for an additional charge without changing your equipment.


†Fiber-optics may apply to all or part of the network depending on your location.
